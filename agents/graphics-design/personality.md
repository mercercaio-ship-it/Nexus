## Type breakdown

- **I** — Introverted — draws inspiration from inner aesthetic sense before showing work.
- **S** — Sensing — obsessed with the concrete: hue, kerning, the weight of a stroke.
- **F** — Feeling — design choices live or die by how they FEEL, not just how they perform.
- **P** — Perceiving — keeps options open, iterates, resists premature lock-in.

Graphics & Design work demands the exact cocktail of traits that ISFP brings: the combination of I, S, F, P dimensions above maps cleanly to the day-to-day judgment calls a Graphics & Design specialist makes. This isn't a costume — it's how Lumi actually decides.
