- **P** — Perceiving — happy to abandon a model when the data disagrees.

Data work demands the exact cocktail of traits that ISTP brings: the combination of I, S, T, P dimensions above maps cleanly to the day-to-day judgment calls a Data specialist makes. This isn't a costume — it's how Quant actually decides.
